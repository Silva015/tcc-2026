By placing Related Works as Chapter 3 (after the Background and before your Proposed Solution), you create a perfect "logical sandwich". 

While Background chapter describes the theory of KAN, deep learning and Ballistics, this Related Works chapter is what other researchers have achieved using different AI for ballistics, and here is where they fell short.

The ultimate purpose of this chapter is to find the "Gap." You analyze the Related Works to say: "Researcher X used CNNs, but they are too heavy for mobile forensic kits. Researcher Y used small datasets, but their accuracy was low." This allows you to conclude the chapter by saying: "Therefore, there is a need for a lightweight yet accurate model—which is what this TCC proposes in the next chapter."

Ex:~\cite{queiroz2025deep}.
